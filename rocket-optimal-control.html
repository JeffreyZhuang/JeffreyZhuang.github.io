Motivation

The rocket launch and landing remains an interesting and challenging control problem as the performance of rockets heavily relies on saving fuel. As a new space race between numerous private organizations is emerging for the cheapest and highest performing launch vehicle, it is increasingly important to exploit optimal control strategies.
